Project Abstract

Our theme is Edu-Tech.
Problem that we have identified: Our goal is to provide quality education to students and this is greatly influenced by the well-trained and highly qualified teachers. Our idea is to bring the schools in a healthy competition environment,a competition of providing quality education to students.

Solution: This will be done through our website where teachers will be tested over performance in various tasks. To address that issue our website will also provide teachers with training (teaching-oriented) of particular subject. Teachers will also be provided with personalized chatbot to access the training material easily.
